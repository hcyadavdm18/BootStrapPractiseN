A Training Management System (TMS) is developed to conduct the training conducted by any company or organization.

It is a software which provide the better service to the company and their employee. The project also provide the details of

employee for company to their process of recruitment. The system can store personal information like experience ,age , position

,address, domain etc. If company want to give training on recent technology then they give notification to all employee of

company. And employee will register if they have interest in the technology. After the registration of all interested candidate

the company HR will see the all applicants and they select the eligible candidate from all register candidate.

And give the message to every selected candidate for the training session.

The system helps to have selections for training to made easy for a company. This project will develop on Asp.net technology only.
